Welcome to AhmedaTreck

Ahmeda: Stand for Ahmedabad

Trek: Stands for moving from one place to another place usually to a short distance

By this project, we will deal with the daily issues of travelling to shorten the distance in a city. This website will help the people of Ahmedabad to rove around the city easily and cheaply. This website is going to benefit both the owner of the vehicle and the person demanding the vehicle. This website will be in two parts, the first one will be for the person who owns the vehicle and the second one for the unknown pillion passengers. This website will contain a search engine and a data box

Search Engine:

The search engine will be made for the unknown pillion passengers (without two wheelers).Here we can search for a vehicle owner who wants to go same place or near to that place.

Data box:

The data box will be made for the owner of the two wheelers having pillion seat empty. Here we can find a person to get our pillion seat full.

Main Motive:

To save money.
To save the resource.
To reduce the traffic in Ahmedabad.
To save petrol.
To minimize the amount of pollution in Ahmedabad.
